About the quiz

This 18-question self-test by pioneering researcher Dr Ivan K Goldberg, highlights signs and symptoms associated with depression. This test is specially made for students, who often suffer from depression or other psychological conditions and still don't know who they should talk to or how they can help themselves. Therefore, the quiz is 100% anonymous and doesn't require any signup. So, what are you waiting for? Take the test TODAY!
